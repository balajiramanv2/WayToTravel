Vietnam Itinerary:

My 7-day trip to Vietnam's itinerary in a nutshell:

  • Day 1: Arrive in Hanoi, check into the hotel, and spend the night there
  • Day 2: Cruise through Halong Bay (lunch, swimming, kayaking, and other activities), overnight on the boat
  • Day 3: Visit Surprising Cave, disembark the ship, land in Hanoi, and spend the night there
  • Day 4: Hoa Lu sightseeing, Ngo Dong river boating, and an overnight stay in Hanoi
  • Day 5: Perfume Pagoda sightseeing and an overnight stay in Hanoi
  • Day 6: Hanoi City Tour; spend the night there
  • 7th day: Departure
